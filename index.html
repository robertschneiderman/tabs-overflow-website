How To Use

Tabs overflow is a very easy to use extension that requires just a few minutes to learn!

Overflow Tab Creation

The Overflow Tab gets created when a 9th tab is opened. Every subsequent tab opening will be "absorbed" by the overflow tab.

Overflow Tab Listing

The Overflow Tab lists all tabs opened after your 8th tab and lets you swap opened tabs and closed tabs by clicking on links

Safe Tabs

Choose which tabs you want to prevent from moving down towards the overflow tab
